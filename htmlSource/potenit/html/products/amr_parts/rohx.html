Rohx

  • Software that allows moving platforms to work autonomously with autonomous driving
  • Apply autonomous driving algorithms based on laser scanners on the mobile platform to enable autonomous driving
  • Auto-driven positioning algorithms based on camera-based positioning algorithms
  • Obtain a map of the driving environment using the sensor (Laser scanner, Smart Camera)
  • Drive route planning and tracking based on obstacle detection, location estimation, and guidance
  • RohX is a PRIMUS program only

Working with RhoX by working environment

Static environment

  • Where the work environment doesn't change
  • Configuring the Autonomous Drive Control System with Laser Scanner

Dynamic environment

  • Places where there are frequent changes in the working environment and dynamic obstacles
  • Laser scanner + Smart camera controlled ride control

Common matters

  • In areas where fixed density of driving position control is required, the system can also be operated in conjunction with tape methods or reflectors, such as tape or reflectors, to enhance the precision of driving precision.
